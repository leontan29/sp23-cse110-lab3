Acme Corporation Board of Directors Meeting

Date: April 20, 2023

Time: 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Location: Acme Corporation Headquarters, Conference Room A

Attendees:

  • John Smith (Chairperson)
  • Jane Adams (CEO)
  • Michael Johnson (CFO)
  • Sarah Lee (COO)
  • Robert Chen (Board Member)
  • Susan Wong (Board Member)

Agenda:

  1. Call to Order
  2. Review of Financial Reports
  3. Strategic Planning Discussion
  4. Board Member Appointments
  5. Other Business
  6. Adjournment

Minutes:

  1. Call to Order

    John Smith called the meeting to order at 9:00 AM and welcomed all attendees.

  2. Review of Financial Reports

    Michael Johnson presented the financial reports for the first quarter of 2023, including the income statement, balance sheet, and cash flow statement. Board members reviewed and discussed the financial reports, noting the positive revenue growth and the need for cost management in certain areas.

    A motion was made by Robert Chen and seconded by Sarah Lee to approve the financial reports. The motion was carried unanimously.

  3. Strategic Planning Discussion

    Jane Adams led a discussion on the strategic planning process for the upcoming fiscal year, highlighting key goals and initiatives. Board members provided feedback and suggestions on the proposed strategic plan, including the need to prioritize customer retention and expansion efforts.

    After thorough discussion, the board agreed to further refine the strategic plan and present it for approval at the next board meeting.

  4. Board Member Appointments

    John Smith informed the board that two board member positions will be vacant due to term expirations and called for nominations for new appointments. Several board members nominated candidates and discussed their qualifications.

    A motion was made by Susan Wong to appoint the nominated candidates as new board members, which was seconded by Michael Johnson. The motion was carried unanimously.

  5. Other Business

    Jane Adams provided updates on recent regulatory changes affecting the company's industry and potential implications. Sarah Lee shared an update on the progress of a new partnership initiative and highlighted its potential benefits. Board members discussed other miscellaneous items, including upcoming events and board member attendance at industry conferences.

    The board discussed and provided feedback on the other business items presented. No motions or decisions were made on these items, as they were for informational purposes only.

  6. Adjournment

    With no further business to discuss, John Smith adjourned the meeting at 11:00 AM.

  7. Optional Items

    Do not show this.
